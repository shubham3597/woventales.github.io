Welcome to Woven Tales

Designer Riteish Singh

Connoted as the ‘man with the golden-touch’; is often spotted with his cup of black coffee while he understands and scribbles requirement of his client. He would make his them ’speak’ unless he is satisfied with all peculiar information to turn a conceived concept to a piece of artistry. All his arrangesments exemplify adroit craftsmanship embraced by sustainable methods and compositions. Through his brand, ‘Woven Tales’ Having successfully completed over seven years in the industry; he has mesmerised the world and muses with his bespoke sustainable-but-wearable fashion creations. A strong believer of ‘power of youth’; the NIFT Alumni is committed to share his learning and knowledge with students at esteemed fashion institutions of the country through Art

His collection ‘viable robes’ helped him gain a lot of recognition in the industry where he produced cent percent organic fibre extracted from milk, soy protein, banana and bamboo residual.
